∞ N & N · Legacy of Love

Ofa2DaMax is a youth-focused nonprofit created in memory of Neko, dedicated to making sure no child gets held back by what they don’t have. Clothing, gear, school needs, basics—whatever it is, the mission is simple: nobody gets left behind on love.

This nonprofit carries Neko’s name and spirit forward. Every act of support, every kid who feels seen, and every bit of love shared in his honor is part of his story living on.

Our Mission

Our mission is to lift children and families with unwavering support, empowerment, and unconditional love. We provide essentials, opportunities, and hope so every child feels seen, valued, and capable of reaching their full potential.

We do this through direct support, school and community partnerships, and sustainable projects that turn apparel and gear into funding for kids.

How We Work

01 · Direct Support

  • Essentials for kids & families
  • Clothing, shoes, school items, hygiene, and basics
  • Fast response when families are in crisis
  • Dignity-first: no shame, no labels

02 · Community

  • School & youth partnerships
  • Referrals from schools, counselors, and coaches
  • Keeping kids connected to school, sports, and peers
  • Focus on kids who feel unseen or last in line

03 · Sustainability

  • Gear with purpose
  • Embroidery & apparel projects with school emblems
  • Proceeds help fund essentials for kids
  • Community pride that gives back
